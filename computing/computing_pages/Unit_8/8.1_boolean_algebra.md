# Boolean Algebra

## Logic Gates

Take one or more inputs and produces a single output.

| Logic Gate| Boolean Algebra | Boolean Notation |
|:---|:---|:---|
| NOT | P = NOT A | P = ¬A |
| AND | P = A AND B | P = A ∧ B |
| OR | P = A OR B | P = A ∨ B |
| XOR | P = A XOR | P = A ⊕ B |
